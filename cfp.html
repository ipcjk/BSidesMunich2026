BSides Munich is a venue for security professionals, software engineers, students, researchers and visionaries. We’re targeting local communities in and around Munich, but everyone from all the corners of the world is welcome. The conference gathers the security community in an event to share and discuss novel ideas, initiatives and advancements. You can see last year’s recordings on youtube and the agenda on our website.

Topics

We are soliciting talks on computer security topics, but are not limited to the following:

  • Network & Security Communications
  • Access & Identity Management
  • Security Operations
  • Software Development Security
  • Security Engineering
  • Risk, Compliance & Security Management
  • Hardware & IoT security
  • Culture, Career, Networking, or Future in the Security Industry
  • AI/ML security
  • Operational technology (OT) security

Submission Types

Talks (30 minutes)

Standard conference presentations on any security topic. All talks are presented in the main conference day in English.

Workshops (2-8 hours)

Hands-on, interactive sessions where participants learn by doing. Workshops take place the Saturdaybefore the conference.

How to Submit

Submissions will be handled through our Pretalx instance. The link will be published here and on our social media channels when the CFP opens.
